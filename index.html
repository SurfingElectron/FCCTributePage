Outer Wilds

Winner of Best Game, Game Design, and Original Property at 2020 BAFTA Game Awards
Named Game of the Year 2019 by Giant Bomb, Polygon, Eurogamer, and The Guardian

"The universe is, and we are."

- Solarnum

The Outer Wilds is a critically acclaimed game about exploring a solar system trapped in time loop. Mysteries await on every planet, the enigmas of an ancient alien civilisation yours to uncover. There is no linear progression in this game; how you explore the system, its secrets and its stories is entirely up to you.

Oh, and don't forget to bring marshmallows.

Development timeline:

  • Late 2012: Alex Beachum begins work on the Outer Wilds as part of his master's thesis at University of Southern California's School of Cinematic Arts' Interactive Media & Games Division
  • March 2015: Wins the Grand Prize at the Independent Games Festival.
  • August 2015: Sources crowdfunding through Fig. The Alpha release is made available for free.
  • March 2018: Recieves funding support from Annapurna Interactive.
  • May 2019: Released for PC and XBox One. A Playstation 4 version follows 6 months later.
  • July 2021: Announcement of an expansion, Echoes of the Eye, which is released 2 months later.
